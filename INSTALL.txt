OPTION 1 — USE ON YOUR PHONE VIA WIFI (Recommended)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Start the server on your PC
  → Double-click "start_server.bat" in this folder
  → Note the IP address shown (e.g. 192.168.1.10)

STEP 2: Open on your phone
  → Make sure phone & PC are on the same WiFi
  → Open your phone's browser
  → Go to: http://YOUR-PC-IP:8080
    (example: http://192.168.1.10:8080)

STEP 3: Install to Home Screen
  iPhone/iPad (Safari):
    → Tap the Share button (⬆ box icon at bottom)
    → Scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen"
    → Tap "Add"

  Android (Chrome):
    → Tap menu (⋮ three dots, top right)
    → Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
    → Tap "Add"

✓ After installing, the app works OFFLINE — no server needed!

Using Netlify Drop (free, no account needed):
  1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop
  2. Drag the entire "RehabPlanner" folder onto the page
  3. You'll get a free URL like: https://random-name.netlify.app
  4. Open that URL on your phone and install to Home Screen
